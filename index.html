The Extremadura Region of Western Spain

Geography of the region

The autonomous community of Extremadura is in western Spain alongside the Portuguese border. It borders the Spanish region of Castilla y leon and Portugal(to the West).

Where to Stay

There is a wide range of accomodation including small inns and guest houses

Climate

Generally Mediterranean, except for the north, where it is continental.

What to see

Spectacular venues include castles,medieval town squares and historic centers.

Gastronomy

Lamb, beef, fabulous cheeses, red and white wines, olive oil.
